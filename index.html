About Me

Passionate About Making a Difference

I am Dr. Erdell Maurice, currently an instructor at Florida A&M University. I completed my Doctoral degree in mathematics education, focused on Curriculum and Instruction with an emphasis in mathematics education. I also hold a Master's degree in Financial Mathematics, two bachelor's degrees in mathematics and actuarial science, and a minor in business.

Originally from Saint Lucia, a small island in the Caribbean, I knew from a very young age that I was destined to do mathematics. I once explained a topic to my class that my teacher struggled to explain — it was an inflection point that taught me the importance of learning complex information and communicating it effectively.

I'm passionate about data science, completing a postgraduate program in AI and Machine Learning focusing on supervised and unsupervised learning, deep learning, computer vision, and natural language processing. My current work focuses on leveraging data science to improve student success.
